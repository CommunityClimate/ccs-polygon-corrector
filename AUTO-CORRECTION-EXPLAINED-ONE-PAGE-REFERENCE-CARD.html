⚡ What Auto-Correction Fixes vs Doesn't Fix

✅ AUTO-CORRECTION FIXES (Simple)

  • Duplicate vertices
    Same point listed twice
  • Coordinate precision
    Too many decimal places
  • Small gaps (<1m)
    Tiny unclosed polygons
  • Point order
    Clockwise vs counter-clockwise
  • Collinear points
    Extra points on straight lines
Why safe: Simple mechanical fixes. No judgment calls. No boundary changes. Average area change: 0.34m² (0.0008%)

❌ AUTO-CORRECTION NEVER FIXES (Complex)

  • Self-intersecting lines
    Boundaries that cross themselves
  • Bow-tie shapes
    Complex geometry errors
  • Overlapping fields
    Multiple fields sharing space
  • Large gaps (>1m)
    Significant unclosed areas
  • Suspicious shapes
    Straight lines, unusual patterns
Why flagged: Requires human judgment. Multiple ways to fix. Business decisions needed. This is where YOU add value!
